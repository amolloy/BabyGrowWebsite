BabyGrow

Version 2.5.3

Track your child's weight, length, and head circumference on your iPhone® or iPod touch®. Charts for these measurements help you visualize your child's progress. Keep an eye on your baby's percentile rankings using data from the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) and the World Health Organization (WHO).

  • Easily keep track of your baby's weight, length, head circumference and BMI.
  • Customizable growth charts help you visualize your child's progress.
  • Track the growth of children up to age 3, and older kids with an in-app purchase.
  • Backup your child's growth data with Dropbox, and rest easy knowing that it is safe.
Available on the iPhone

Requirements: Compatible with iPhone and iPod touch. Requires iPhone OS 6.0 or later. Network connection required for Dropbox backup.

Is BabyGrow Accurate?

BabyGrow uses data obtained directly from the CDC and WHO to calculate percentiles for measurements. We use an exhaustive test suite which ensures the percentiles BabyGrow calculates match those calculated by software provided by the CDC and WHO.

Not Convinced?

Try BabyCalc, a free app for calculating percentiles for children up to three months of age. BabyCalc shares the same calculation engine as BabyGrow, and includes the same data from the CDC and WHO. Check it out!

Apps for Parents, By Parents

I began working on BabyGrow while my wife was pregnant with our first son. Since then, I've used the app myself to keep track of the growth of my own children. Because I am familiar with every facet of the app, not only as a developer but also as a user and a parent, I am able to identify what about the app works well, and what doesn't. I strive to use that knowledge to improve BabyGrow, to make it the best app of its kind.

BabyGrow is an app for parents, developed by parents. I do the design and development, and my wife provides feedback, support, and ideas.

My wife also runs a blog about creative ways to play with your kids, yellowpop. Please check it out!
