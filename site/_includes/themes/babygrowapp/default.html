Track your baby's weight, length, and head circumference on your iPhone™ or iPod touch®. Charts for these measurements help you visualize your child's progress. Keep an eye on your baby's percentile rankings using data from the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) or the World Health Organization (WHO).

  • Monitor the progress of an unlimited number of children, from birth to 36 months.
  • Produce growth charts for Weight for Age, Length for Age, Head Circumference for Age and Weight for Length.
  • Understand your child's growth patterns with accurate percentile information from the CDC and WHO.
  • Instantly obtain percentiles for measurements for babies of any age from birth to 36 months.
  • Supports pounds and kilograms for weight, and inches and centimeters for length.
  • Send emails containing your babies' data.
  • Backup your data using Dropbox.
