Track your baby's weight, length, and head circumference on your iPhone™ or iPod touch®. Charts for these measurements help you visualize your child’s progress. Keep an eye on your baby's percentile rankings using data from the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) or the World Health Organization (WHO).
